Egyptian Music

Egyptian music is an integral part of the country's culture. Some key genres and instruments include:

  • Shaabi - A genre of popular music that evolved from Egyptian folk music.
  • Classical Arabic music - Traditional music played with instruments such as the oud and qanun.
  • Modern Egyptian pop - Contemporary music blending Western and traditional sounds.
  • Instruments - Oud, Ney, Qanun, Tabla.
