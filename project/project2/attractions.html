Egyptian Attractions

Egypt is home to some of the world's most famous historical sites and natural wonders, including:

  • The Pyramids of Giza - The last of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
  • The Sphinx - A limestone statue with the body of a lion and the head of a pharaoh.
  • Luxor Temple - A large Ancient Egyptian temple complex located on the east bank of the Nile River.
  • The Valley of the Kings - A valley where tombs were constructed for the Pharaohs and powerful nobles of the New Kingdom.
